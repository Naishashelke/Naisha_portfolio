About Me

I am an Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning enthusiast with a strong background in computer science research. My work focuses on developing intelligent systems using machine learning, computer vision, and advanced AI algorithms. I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Information Science and Engineering from Presidency University in Bangalore, India. I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Computer Science and Information Engineering at National Chung Cheng University in Chiayi, Taiwan. My research interests include AI-driven healthcare systems, federated learning, robotics perception, and intelligent computer vision models. I am passionate about research and development and aspire to contribute to innovative AI technologies that solve real-world challenges.
