Application of Novel Snapshot-based Hyperspectral Imaging Combined with AI to Detect Skin Lesions

This research focuses on the application of artificial intelligence and snapshot-assisted visual enhancement (SAVE) hyperspectral imaging for the detection and classification of melanoma skin cancer.

Five hybrid machine learning models were developed and evaluated to improve classification performance for melanoma and its subtypes. The study compares model performance using metrics such as accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score.

Status: Paper currently under review in the journal Skin Research and Technology.

Research Area: Artificial Intelligence, Medical Imaging, Computer Vision, Healthcare AI

Breast Cancer Detection using Convolutional Neural Networks

This research presents a deep learning-based approach for detecting breast cancer using medical imaging datasets.

A convolutional neural network model was developed to classify tumor images and improve early-stage detection accuracy. The work focuses on improving reliability of automated medical diagnosis systems using artificial intelligence.

Journal: International Journal of Scientific Research and Engineering Development (IJSRED)

ISSN: 2581-7175

Research Area: Deep Learning, Medical Imaging, Computer Vision, Healthcare AI
